Frequently asked
Is my data private?
Yes. All calculations run entirely in your browser. No financial data is ever sent to our servers — we never see your numbers.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your account settings and your subscription ends at the current billing period.
Is this financial advice?
No. RetirementPlanner is an educational tool for exploring scenarios and trade-offs. Always consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and Apple Pay & Google Pay on supported devices. Payments processed securely by Stripe.
Will prices change?
Current pricing is locked in for all active subscribers. If we ever change pricing, existing subscribers keep their current rate.
