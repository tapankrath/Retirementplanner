Frequently asked
Which pricing should I choose — USD or INR?
If you live and work in the US (even if planning to retire in India), choose USD. INR pricing is for users currently in India who primarily use the India planning tools. The Retire in India cross-border tool is included in both plans.
Is my data private?
Yes — all calculations run entirely in your browser. No financial data is ever sent to our servers. We never see your numbers.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your account settings and your subscription ends at the current billing period.
Is this financial advice?
No. RetirementPlanner is an educational tool for exploring scenarios and trade-offs. Always consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and UPI for INR payments. Processed securely via Stripe.
Will prices change?
Current pricing is locked for all active subscribers. If we ever change pricing, existing subscribers keep their current rate.
